NoteKeeper

Role: Fullstack Developer
Technologies Xcode 11

NoteKeeper is an iOS app that allows students to store or type their notes in their phone. When sharing their notes with another classmate, they can easily find it on the app because it’ll be organized by class name. This app also isn’t limited to class notes - students can store homework questions/solutions, and study guide solutions as well.

As a fullstack developer, I first designed the app through Figma, and then used Xcode to bring the idea to life. The interface builder in Xcode allowed me to add buttons, pictures, transitions, and many more features to my app.

Through this project, I was able to take a peek into the world of mobile app developing. This project also gave me the opportunity to learn more about user experience and usability through a smartphone perspective, and not just through a desktop lens.
