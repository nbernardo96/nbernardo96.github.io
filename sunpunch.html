Sunpunch

Role: Frontend React Developer
Technologies used: React, NodeJS, Express, KnexJS

Sunpunch is a site dedicated to help manage employees working from home.

With the transition of living in a world where masks and social distancing are important, companies are starting to adapt and implement the process of working from home. One of the problems that may occur for businesses when working from home is finding a way to stay organized when employees start and end their shifts. This project aims to tackle that issue. The goal of Sunpunch is to help prevent time fraud and accurately report punch in and punch out times to the schedule manager/payroll employee. As a frontend web developer, I designed and implemented the login, signup, and dashboard pages for this project.
